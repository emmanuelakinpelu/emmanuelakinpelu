About Me

Akinpelu Gbenga Emmanuel(Emakins) is a seasoned ICT professional with over 10 years of experience in the technology industry. He has built a distinguished career in four major roles: Network and Systems Specialist, ICT Educator and Consultant, Data and Web Solutions Specialist, and Hardware Technician. With a solid foundation in global certifications and hands-on expertise, Akinpelu has consistently delivered innovative and reliable ICT solutions across multiple sectors. Over the years, Akinpelu has held various positions in different organizations, enhancing his expertise in both technical and ICT management roles. Currently, he serves as the ICT Standard Evaluation Officer at Emmakulate System Solutions, where he oversees ICT evaluation and standardization. He is also the former Director of an ICT school and began his career as an ICT Instructor and System Engineer, roles that laid the foundation for his growth in the field. In 2013, Akinpelu worked as the Assistant Head of Department (ICT) at Sapade Polytechnic, where he played a key role in training students in ICT-related courses. During the same period, he served as a part-time lecturer at the Institute of Business Technology Management of Nigeria, sharing his expertise with future ICT professionals. Early in his career, he gained experience in various roles, including Data Entry Officer, Graphic Designer, Hardware and Networking Technician, and Web Administrator. These positions enriched his skill set and gave him a holistic understanding of the ICT ecosystem. Network and Systems Specialist Akinpelu is a certified Cisco Network Associate (CCNA) and Microsoft Certified Solutions Associate, with extensive experience in designing, deploying, and managing IT infrastructure. His expertise includes network engineering, system administration, and implementing secure environments for organizations, such as the Nigerian Air Force and various educational institutions. ICT Educator and Consultant As a passionate educator and consultant, Akinpelu has trained students, professionals, and organizations in essential ICT skills, ranging from Microsoft Office applications to robotics programming (Arduino and Scratch), web design, and networking. He has developed comprehensive training materials and provided consultancy services to schools, businesses, and military organizations, ensuring successful ICT project implementation and skill development. Data and Web Solutions Specialist Proficient in data analysis, web development, and cloud technologies, Akinpelu delivers cutting-edge solutions to businesses and organizations. Currently pursuing AWS Cloud Data Engineering certification, he leverages data-driven insights and modern web technologies to enhance operational efficiency and drive business growth. Hardware Technician With extensive experience in diagnosing, troubleshooting, and maintaining hardware systems, Akinpelu is an accomplished hardware technician. His hands-on expertise includes system maintenance, hardware upgrades, and configuring ICT labs for schools and enterprises. Akinpelu Gbenga Emmanuel is passionate about driving technological advancement, sharing knowledge, and solving complex problems through innovative solutions. His ability to align with global ICT standards and adapt to evolving trends makes him an invaluable asset to any organization seeking to thrive in a technology-driven world.
